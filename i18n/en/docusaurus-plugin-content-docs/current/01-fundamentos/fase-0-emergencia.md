# Phase 0: Humanitarian Emergency (First 180 Days)

:::danger Absolute Priority
No citizen will believe in a sovereign fund if they don't have water, food, or medicine. 80% of the population below the poverty line. The [UN raised only 17%](https://www.cgdev.org/blog/barreling-blindly-ahead-seizure-venezuelas-oil) of its USD 600M appeal.
:::

|----------|--------|---------|-----------|

## Sprint 0: The First 100 Days

:::tip Rule of political survival
If there are no visible results in 100 days, the plan is politically dead. There is no second chance. — Bukele
:::

The first 100 days determine whether the plan has credibility or is just another document no one executes. Every action here is a **signal** — to the citizen, to the investor, to the diaspora, to the world. It's not about solving everything. It's about proving that **this is different**.

> For the full timeline of the first 1,000 days with 100-day sprints → see [Timeline: First 1,000 Days](/07-ejecucion/timeline#first-1000-days-sprints-with-visible-results)

### Day 1-30: Emergency + Signal to the World

| # | Action | Concrete Deliverable | Signal It Sends |
|---|--------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Activate 3 hospitals** with international medical teams (PAHO/MSF) | 3 operational hospitals in Caracas, Maracaibo, Valencia with supplies, operating rooms, staff | "People first — not oil" |
| 2 | **Deploy Starlink in 5 ZEET cities** (Caracas, Valencia, Barquisimeto, Maracaibo, Pto. Ordaz) | 50+ free internet access points (plazas, schools, hospitals) at 100+ Mbps | "Venezuela connects to the world in 30 days, not 5 years" |
| 3 | **Launch transparency dashboard** (web + mobile, even if basic) | Public platform with: budget, expenditures, project progress, contracts | "Every dollar is tracked. No more FONDEN." |
| 4 | **Sign first forward contract** (Chevron expansion or new operator) | USD 1-3B in committed advances. Public announcement with exact figure | "The money is already coming in" |
| 5 | **Announce formal dollarization** + monetary stabilization plan | Decree + 180-day roadmap for monetary transition | "Four-digit inflation is over" |
| 6 | **Declare security emergency** in 5 cities with immediate deployment | 24/7 safe pilot zones. International cooperation activated | "Walk safely at night — in 30 days" |

### Day 31-60: Visible Infrastructure Sprint

| # | Action | Concrete Deliverable | Signal It Sends |
|---|--------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **First road repaired** — visible, symbolic, high-traffic | Fully rehabilitated stretch (e.g.: Caracas-La Guaira or Maracaibo highway) | "Things get fixed. You can see them. You can touch them." |
| 2 | **First ZEET coworking space opened** (100 seats, Starlink + fiber) | Operational space with internet, electricity, air conditioning — the standard to come | "Venezuela is open for tech" |
| 3 | **Reformed police pilot in 1 city** (Georgia model) | New police in 1 city: new uniforms, fair salaries, zero criminal records | "You don't reform the police — you replace it" |
| 4 | **Open banking regulation published** | Regulatory framework for fintech, digital payments, mobile banking | "Venezuela will be cashless in 5 years" |
| 5 | **First multilateral agreement** (IDB/CAF/World Bank) | Loan or grant for basic infrastructure. Public figure | "The world is betting on Venezuela" |

### Day 61-100: First Visible Returns

| # | Action | Concrete Deliverable | Signal It Sends |
|---|--------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **First citizen investment possible** — MVP app or web portal | Citizens can invest USD 10-100 in Venezuela S.A. bonds from their phone | "You're a shareholder in your country" |
| 2 | **First bootcamp launches** (500 students, software development) | Cohort 1 underway: 500 students in 3 cities, tech curriculum, full scholarships | "From street vendor to developer in 6 months" |
| 3 | **Data center feasibility study** completed with a major (Google/Microsoft/Amazon) | Public document with location, cost, timeline, energy commitment | "Tech giants are looking at Venezuela" |
| 4 | **Dashboard shows first results** | Public metrics: X active hospitals, Y km of roads, Z direct jobs created | "Numbers don't lie and they're visible to everyone" |
| 5 | **First diaspora return program** launched | Incentives: fast-track visa, 3-year tax exemption, housing assistance. Active digital census | "Come back. There's a reason to." |

### 100-Day Scorecard

| Metric | Day 100 Target | How It's Measured |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Operational hospitals | **3+** | PAHO/MSF verification |
| Public internet access points | **50+** | Public speed tests |
| Committed investment (forwards) | **USD 1-3B** | Signed contracts |
| Direct jobs created | **5,000+** | Payroll records |
| Homicides in pilot zones | **-30% vs. baseline** | Police data + NGOs |
| Public dashboard operational | **Yes/No** | Verifiable URL |
| Citizens registered as investors | **10,000+** | Digital platform |

:::danger The 100 days are the plan's pitch deck
An investor decides in 10 minutes whether to put in money. A citizen decides in 100 days whether to believe in the plan. Everything that happens in this sprint is Venezuela's pitch deck to the world. If it's convincing, the USD 183B will come. If not, the memes will come.
:::
