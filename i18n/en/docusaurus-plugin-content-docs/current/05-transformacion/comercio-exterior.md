# Foreign Trade and Bilateral Agreements

> A diversified economy needs markets. Venezuela has been commercially isolated for over a decade.

## Diagnosis

Venezuela was suspended from MERCOSUR in 2017. It has active sanctions from the U.S. and the EU. Its main buyers are intermediaries (India, China) rather than direct customers.

| Problem | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| MERCOSUR suspension | No preferential access to Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay |
| U.S. sanctions | PDVSA on OFAC list, banks without correspondent banking |
| EU sanctions | Restrictions on officials and entities |
| No active bilateral investment treaties (BITs) | Investors without legal protection |
| Dependence on intermediaries | India and China buy crude at a discount |

## Commercial Reintegration Plan

### Phase 1: Normalization (Years 1-3)

| Action | Benefit | Condition |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Progressive sanctions relief | Market access, SWIFT, financing | Elections + transition |
| MERCOSUR reentry | Access to a 300 M person market | Compliance with democratic protocol |
| Bilateral investment treaties (BITs) | Legal protection for investors | Judicial reform (see [Rule of Law](/04-gobernanza/estado-derecho-moneda)) |
| Direct oil agreements | Eliminate intermediaries, improve pricing | Buyer diversification |

### Phase 2: Expansion (Years 3-7)

| Action | Target market | Potential |
|--------|--------------|-----------|
| Energy agreement with Colombia | Natural gas + electricity | USD 1,000+ M/year (see [Diversification](/05-transformacion/diversificacion)) |
| LNG agreement with Trinidad and Tobago | Natural gas → LNG export | USD 2,000-4,000 M/year |
| Tech agreement with Chile/Uruguay | Digital services, startups | Tech ecosystem |
| Caribbean: tourism + food agreement | Tourism + agroindustry | USD 2,000+ M/year |
| Venezuelan RIGI | Foreign investment >USD 200 M with 30-year stability | [Argentina RIGI](https://www.upi.com/Top_News/World-News/2025/10/30/bcpargentina-RIGI-foreign-invetments-report/1561761834454/) model |

### Phase 3: Regional Leadership (Years 7-15)

| Action | Vision |
|--------|--------|
| Caribbean-Venezuela free trade zone | Venezuela as Caribbean logistics hub |
| Post-sanctions EU agreements | Tech, cacao, tourism exports |
| LATAM energy integration | Electricity + LNG exports to the region |

:::info RIGI lesson (Argentina under Milei)
Argentina's [RIGI regime](https://www.upi.com/Top_News/World-News/2025/10/30/bcpargentina-RIGI-foreign-invetments-report/1561761834454/) offers: 25% corporate tax (vs. 35%), 30-year regulatory stability, international arbitration. Projections: [USD 33,900 M in investments](https://www.upi.com/Top_News/World-News/2025/10/30/bcpargentina-RIGI-foreign-invetments-report/1561761834454/) over 5-10 years. Venezuela's ZEETs can adopt a similar model with even more aggressive benefits (0% tax for 10 years).
:::
