# Education Redesigned for the AI Era

[WEF](https://www.weforum.org/publications/the-future-of-jobs-report-2025/): 39% of skills will become obsolete 2025-2030. 85% of employers prioritize reskilling.

| Level | Reform | Investment |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| Primary | Computational thinking + English from 1st grade | USD 2-4,000 M |
| Secondary | Programming + statistics + technical-vocational track | USD 3-5,000 M |
| Technical | 6-12 month certifications (AI, solar, cyber). Google/AWS partnerships | USD 1-2,000 M |
| University | 3 KAIST/Technion-type institutes. Merit-based scholarships | USD 5-8,000 M |
| Adult reskilling | 500,000/year in 3-6 month courses + income during retraining | USD 2-3,000 M/year |

**Total education:** USD 15,000-25,000 M over 15 years
