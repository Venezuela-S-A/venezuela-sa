# AI Impact: Getting Ahead of the Transformation

[WEF Future of Jobs 2025](https://www.weforum.org/publications/the-future-of-jobs-report-2025/): 170 M jobs created, 92 M displaced by 2030. [McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com): generative AI can automate 60-70% of activities.

[UNICEF/Innocenti (2026)](https://www.unicef.org/innocenti/stories/2026-global-outlook-reshaping-work-ai-driven-labour-market): nearly half of 17 M LATAM jobs that could benefit from AI are limited by poor infrastructure. **Opportunity:** rebuild infrastructure natively for AI.

## Jobs at Risk by Sector

| Sector | Risk | Mitigation |
|--------|------|------------|
| Banking/finance | High (40-60%) | Reskilling → fintech |
| Public administration | High (50%+) | Digital government |
| Retail | Medium-High | E-commerce + experience |
| Tourism | Low | Caribbean experience as differentiator |
| Healthcare | Low | AI as tool, not substitute |

| Cybersecurity | Top 3 | Exportable service |
