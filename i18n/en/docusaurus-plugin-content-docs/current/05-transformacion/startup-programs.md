# Venezuela Emprende: Acceleration Ecosystem

Inspired by [Start-Up Chile](https://startupchile.org/en/) (5,000+ alumni, 85 countries, USD 1,000 M+ in sales, USD 1,200 M+ raised) and [CORFO](https://www.corfo.cl) (USD 1.061 B across 72 VC funds, 1998-2024).

| Program | Model | Funding | Focus |
|---------|-------|---------|-------|
| Semilla Venezuela | Start-Up Chile Build | USD 10-20K equity-free | Ideas at MVP stage |
| Ignite Venezuela | Start-Up Chile Ignite | USD 25-50K equity-free | Product-market fit |
| Escala Venezuela | YCombinator/Techstars | USD 100-250K (7-10% equity) | Traction + global projection |
| National VC Fund | CORFO VC | USD 50-200 M matching | Series A/B |

## Dual Priority: AI-First + AI-Resistant

**Category A (AI-first, top priority):** SaaS with AI for LATAM, AI applied to energy, AgTech, HealthTech, EdTech, FinTech, cybersecurity

**Category B (AI-resistant, high human value):** Caribbean experiential tourism, premium gastronomy, artisanal crafts, healthcare, specialized trades

**Goal:** Formalize 1 million micro-businesses in 5 years (registration in 24 hours + 2 years at 0% tax)
