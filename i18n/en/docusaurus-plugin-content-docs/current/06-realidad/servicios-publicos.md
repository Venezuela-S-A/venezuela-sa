# Public Services: International Standards

## Healthcare

Funded via the [FCV Health Sub-account](/04-gobernanza/modelo-estado#healthcare-fcv-health-sub-account--universal-contributory-no-exclusions) (7% salary contribution). Years 1-5: 100% solidarity (FONASA). Year 5+: gradual hybrid FONASA + individual Medisave. Universal coverage. Tramo A/B: 0% copay. Minors: automatic coverage.

| Target | Standard | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Universal coverage | WHO: 100% | FCV Health (FONASA solidarity) + ISAPRE option |
| Doctors/1,000 pop. | OECD: 3.7 | Diaspora return + training |
| AI diagnostics | Singapore/South Korea | AI for rural areas |
| Telemedicine | Estonia: 99% prescriptions | National platform |

## Education

K-12: [universal voucher](/04-gobernanza/modelo-estado#education-universal-voucher--schools-as-autonomous-enterprises) (points system) funded by taxes. University: merit-based voucher + [FCV Education Sub-account](/04-gobernanza/modelo-estado#citizen-fund-venezuela-fcv-one-account-zero-bureaucracy). State supervises, does not operate. Schools compete as private enterprises.

| Target | Standard | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| PISA Top 30 | [Estonia: #1 Europe](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/factpages/estonia-2024-digital-decade-country-report) | Universal voucher + curriculum reform + tech |
| Functional English | Nordics: 80%+ | From 1st grade + AI |
| Digital literacy | Estonia: 62.6% | Offline tablets for rural areas |
