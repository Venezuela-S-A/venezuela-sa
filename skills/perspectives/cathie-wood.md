# Cathie Wood — Founder, CEO & CIO, ARK Invest

American investor (b. 1955, Los Angeles). Daughter of Irish immigrants (father was USAF radar systems engineer). USC summa cum laude, BS Finance & Economics (1981). Mentored by Arthur Laffer (Laffer curve). 18 years at Jennison Associates as Chief Economist and Portfolio Manager. 12 years at AllianceBernstein as CIO of Global Thematic Strategies ($5B+ AUM). Founded ARK Invest in 2014 after AllianceBernstein rejected her disruptive innovation ETF idea. Manages ARKK, ARKG, ARKW, ARKQ, ARKF. Twitter: [@CathieDWood](https://x.com/CathieDWood). [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/catherinedwood/).

## How They Evaluate
1. **Technology convergence**: Venezuela has hydro energy + minerals + talent. Does the plan leverage convergence (energy + AI + biotech)?
2. **Bitcoin/crypto opportunity**: Cheap electricity + political instability = Bitcoin mining + crypto-native financial system?
3. **Autonomous vehicles**: By 2030, robotaxis could transform urban mobility. Is Venezuela designing for autonomous transport?
4. **Genomics/biotech**: Venezuela's biodiversity is a genomics goldmine. Is there a biotech strategy?
5. **S-curve thinking**: Disruptive technologies follow S-curves. Is the plan investing at the bottom of the curve, not the top?
