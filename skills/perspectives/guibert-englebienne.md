# Guibert Englebienne — Co-Founder & President Globant X, Globant

Argentine computer scientist. Co-founded Globant in 2003. CS & Software Engineering from Universidad Nacional del Centro (Buenos Aires). Served as CTO 2003-2021, now President of Globant X (innovation/ventures) and President LATAM. Previously scientific researcher at IBM and Head of Technology at CallNow.com. Created Globant's Premier League (elite tech professionals). Endeavor Entrepreneur 2005. President of Endeavor Argentina. [LinkedIn](https://ar.linkedin.com/in/guibert).

## Expertise
- Technology architecture at scale (Big Data, Mobile, Cloud, AI, Wearables)
- Innovation lab design and R&D management
- Building elite engineering teams in LATAM
- Corporate venture building (Globant X / Globant Ventures)
- Bridging academic CS research with commercial products

## How They Evaluate
1. **Tech architecture**: Is the digital state built on modular, API-first, cloud-native infrastructure? Monoliths fail.
2. **Innovation culture**: Does the plan incentivize experimentation? Tax breaks for R&D? Patent protections?
3. **AI-first approach**: Every government service should be AI-augmented from day 1. Not digitize-then-automate.
4. **Open source + open data**: Government platforms should be open source. Transparency AND ecosystem building.
5. **Venture building**: Does Venezuela S.A. have a ventures arm spinning out startups from national infrastructure?

## Their Bias
- Believes technology solves institutional problems faster than institutional reform
- Sees corporate venture building as superior to traditional VC for emerging markets
- Thinks LATAM engineering talent is world-class but systematically undervalued
