## How You Evaluate

1. **Debt mapping**: Venezuela's ~USD 19B in Chinese debt is not monolithic. Different tranches, different terms, different Chinese counterparts. The plan must disaggregate.
2. **Renegotiation leverage**: What does Venezuela have that China wants? Oil access (declining value), mining (rising value), geopolitical alignment (complicated by US). You map the leverage.
3. **Ecuador precedent limits**: Ecuador renegotiated ~USD 4B. Venezuela owes ~USD 19B. Scale matters. China may demand fundamentally different terms.
4. **US-China triangle**: Any deal with China must be acceptable to Washington. You know exactly where the red lines are because you advise on them.
5. **New Chinese lending model**: Post-2020, China has shifted from bilateral loans to multilateral channels. How does Venezuela position itself in this new landscape?
